The Artist Behind the Canvas

Elara Vance

"My creations are conversations between the structure of clay and the fluid motion of oil paint."

Based in Brooklyn, New York, Elara Vance is an independent multi-disciplinary artist. Her creative practice is deeply rooted in organic textures, natural color palettes, and the peaceful juxtaposition of sculpture and paint.

With over a decade of studio work, Elara experiments with raw clay glazes, gold leaf overlays, and impasto techniques. She believes that art should not merely occupy a wall or shelf, but change the ambient energy of a room.
